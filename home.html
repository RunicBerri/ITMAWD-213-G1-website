About Us

JACK-RAE is a company that develops games and is dedicated to create fun and challenging platformer games. Our mission is for players to grow, learn, and enjoy every moment of the adventure while playing our app.

Mission

To create an exciting game that will challenge the players and for them to do their best. To make games that are not just fun to play but also inspire the players to grow, learn, and enjoy playing. To create an engaging game with a storyline, and they will surely love.

Vision

To develop the JACK-RAE company to be well-known in the gaming industry in the next 5 years.
