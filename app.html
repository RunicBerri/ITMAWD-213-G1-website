About App

Long ago, the Sakura Bonsai were kept safe by the Order Gems, and by keeping the Sakura Bonsai safe, the dungeon was free to be explore by travelers and adventures alike. However, one unfortunate day, the Order Gems were stolen from the Sakura bonsai, and thus, the dungeon fell into chaos. Traps and monsters appeared to guard the now powerless Sakura bonsai against all who dared to enter, regardless of foe or an ally. But after a few decades, the Order Gems were gathered again and are now planned to be returned to the Sakura Bonsai. Those who gathered the Order Gems appointed Jhin with the task to return the said gems to their respective Sakura Bonsai. Jhin was appointed because of his skill in traversing dangerous terrain and obstacles. Before Jhin left his town, he was awarded the title "Gemkeeper" thus starting quest to return the Order Gems.
